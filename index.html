ℹ About Departments

Departments are used in Model Master → Operations to assign each activity to a work area (Assembly, Paint, Quality, Electrical etc.).


They also appear in Job Card print for filtering and in Gantt View for colour coding by department.


The colour you set here will be used throughout the app for that department's bars, badges, and charts.

ℹ About Operators

Operators appear in Job Card creation — assign a person to each selected operation.


In Log Progress, the operator field shows a dropdown from this master so names are consistent.


Reports show efficiency and hours per operator based on their progress log entries.

ℹ About Delay Responsibilities

When a delay is logged in Log Progress, you select who is responsible — this drives accountability and reporting.


Examples: SCM (material shortage), Maintenance (machine breakdown), Design (drawing change), Production (operator error).


The Reports → Delays tab shows a pareto of delay hours by responsibility.
